Fashion Design Program

This three-year co-operative Ontario College Advanced Diploma prepares students for success in the fashion and apparel industry through hands-on training in design, production, and marketing.

Develop skills in fashion illustration, computer-aided design (CAD), pattern-making, draping, and garment construction while gaining real-world experience through labs, field trips, and work placements. Graduate with a professional portfolio and the confidence to launch your future in fashion.
