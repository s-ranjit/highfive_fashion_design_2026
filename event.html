Hero Section

THEME:
Tapestry

Tapestry represents many distinct voices existing together without being flattened into a single aesthetic. Each collection acts as its own thread — shaped by personal narrative, cultural influence, material exploration, and design intention. When viewed together, these threads form a unified body of work that reflects both individuality and shared process. The result is a collective experience that honors difference while celebrating connection.
